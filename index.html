About Aridity Team

The Aridity Team is a group of developers who mainly work with .NET (VB and C#) or C++. We also develop games based on the Source engine.

If you're interested in joining us, you don't need to be a software developer/engineer. We welcome contributions in sound design, graphics, voice acting, and more!
